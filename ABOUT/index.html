About us

According to a research of online-sales, no seller can last hot sale of 1 item for 6 months, as even the top 3 sellers only get an average of 98.9% positive vote, and 1 bad review takes 57 good to cover its negative impact, it hinders the growth, and that also gives opportunity to new companies/competitors to start, which makes the existing market share worse.

The main reason is almost all companies still use the same quality process and criteria with traditional orders: quality assessed based on the entire order. But online-selling actually requires high quality with strong packaging for each single piece.

The founder runs a non-profit project: www.FactoryABC.com, feedback is that it helps but takes time to follow up.

So he established NewlyTop Co.,Ltd. focusing on Mail-order solution to serve some busy E-sales clients directly with 3 factories chosen from thousands of inspections, and quality controlled throughout by CNS (China Neutral Service), a professional 3rd party quality control company, over below processes:

  • Quality:

    1. Pre-production sample full check;
    2. Material check and inline semi-product inspection;
    3. Full inspection with 1 piece by 1 piece before packaging;
    4. Final inspection after finish, if result is PASS, release; if any issue, then:
    5. Unpack all mass product, re-order all packaging;
    6. 1 piece by 1 piece inspection before re-packaging;
    7. final inspection, if PASS, ship out; otherwise re-produce all, and same quality control process again;
    8. if problem again, cancel the order with full refund.

  • Packaging: besides ISTA 1A carton drop test, additional abuse test of transportation to:

    1. Pre-production sample, proceed mass production after pass;
    2. Finished products, if pass, release; otherwise, strengthen packaging until pass.
